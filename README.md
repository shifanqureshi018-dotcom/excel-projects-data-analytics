## 1. Amazon Sales Report Analysis
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

**Overview:** Analyzed 3,500+ Amazon orders to uncover revenue
trends, top categories, and high-performing states.

**Key Findings:**
- Total Revenue: ₹23.2L | Avg Order Value: ₹625 | Units Sold: 3,582
- Top Categories: Set, Kurta (highest sales volume)
- Top States: Maharashtra, Karnataka (40%+ of total revenue)
- Recommendation: Focus inventory on top 2 states to reduce stockouts

## 2. Auto MPG Fuel Efficiency Analysis
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Seaborn, Correlation Analysis

**Overview:** Analyzed 392 vehicle records to study fuel efficiency
patterns across weight, horsepower, and cylinder count.

**Key Findings:**
- MPG vs Weight correlation: -0.83 (strong negative)
- MPG vs Horsepower correlation: -0.78 (strong negative)
- 4-cylinder cars average 29.3 MPG vs 8-cylinder at 15.0 MPG
- Recommendation: Fleet procurement should prioritize vehicles
  under 2500 lbs for 30%+ fuel savings
